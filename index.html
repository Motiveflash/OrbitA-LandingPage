About OrbitA

OrbitA is a centralized platform designed to empower Africa’s technology ecosystem by connecting tech hubs, fostering innovation, and driving collaboration across the continent. With initiatives like cross-border project incubation, an extensive mentorship network, shared investment opportunities, and a comprehensive tech knowledge repository, OrbitA aims to unify African tech talents and resources. The platform supports young entrepreneurs, startups, and established businesses through tailored programs and access to cutting-edge tools, creating a sustainable and inclusive future for Africa. Explore opportunities, join our tech-driven community, and be part of Africa’s digital transformation with OrbitA.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a unified tech ecosystem that empowers innovation, fosters collaboration, and drives sustainable growth across Africa. We believe in bridging gaps, connecting communities, and unlocking potential through cutting-edge technology, robust partnerships, and visionary leadership. By aligning resources, nurturing talent, and facilitating cross-border collaboration, we aim to inspire progress and build a better, more inclusive future for all Africans.

Core Values:

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.
  • Collaboration: Building strong connections that inspire growth.
  • Sustainability: Creating lasting impact through tech-driven solutions.
  • Inclusivity: Ensuring no one is left behind in Africa’s tech journey.

Our Vision

At OrbitA, we envision a thriving, interconnected African tech community that unlocks the continent's full potential. We aim to be the leading force driving innovation, collaboration, and inclusive growth, transforming Africa into a global technology powerhouse.

Key Elements of Our Vision:

  • A continent-wide network bridging tech hubs, startups, and innovators to accelerate progress.

  • Providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for the youth, entrepreneurs, and businesses to thrive.

  • Leveraging technology to address Africa’s unique challenges in healthcare, education, agriculture, and more.

  • Showcasing Africa's talent and solutions on the global stage, ensuring African voices lead in shaping the future of technology.

Key Features

  • Unified Tech Ecosystem

    Connects tech hubs across Africa, creating a single platform for collaboration, innovation, and resource sharing.

  • Cross-Border Collaboration

    Enables seamless partnerships across countries, fostering the development of scalable solutions for Africa-wide challenges.

  • OrbitA Passport Membership

    A membership program offering access to partner/connected hubs, resources, and mentorship across the continent.

  • Knowledge Repository

    Centralized data and resources, including research, success stories, and case studies to empower all hubs.

  • Pan-African Investment Fund

    A shared funding initiative to support startups and projects addressing African challenges through tech innovation.

  • Hub Mentorship Program

    Pairs well-established hubs with smaller hubs to share expertise and accelerate growth across the network.

  • Tech Innovation Summit

    Annual events bringing together tech talent, investors, and innovators to showcase and celebrate African ingenuity.

  • Open-Source Collaboration

    A platform for sharing and developing open-source solutions tailored for the African context.
